A refreshing, simple smoothie for any occasion

Description

A low effort smoothie that will satisfy most while requiring no particular ingredients except a few basics.

Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas
  • 500 grams of frozen fruit or berries
  • juice of 1/2 a lemon
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 5 dl of milk

Steps

  1. In a blender, add all ingredients
  2. Bon appetite!
