Nice and simple hummus you can make any day

Description

This hummus requires little to no effort or time, and is miles better than any store bought hummus at your local supermarket.

Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp Tahini
  • Half a lemon of lemon juice
  • 1 can of chickpeas
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 dash of ground cayenne pepper
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. In a blender, add tahini, cumin, cayenne pepper, lemon juice, olive oil and garlic and if it doesn't blend, add aqua faba from the chickpea can.
  2. Add chickpeas.
  3. Add salt and pepper for taste.
  4. Bon appetite!
